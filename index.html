What is Te Mata?

Te Mata is one of the four houses at Karamu High School, named after the local landmark Te Mata Peak. With a brazen orange brand and a thriving community of competitive students, Te Mata has won several house sport events. Some of these include:

  • Cross-country
  • Swmming Sports
  • Athletics
